Advertising

One of the core component of advertising is the effectiveness of communication of the product or offering.

It should be designed in such a way that it should be memorable and influential.


Purpose of Advertising:
Increase awareness 
Improve potential customers
To promote sales
To introduce a new service
To differentiate your product


Online advertising 
Ad serving describes the technology and service that places advertisements on web sites. It uses the Internet and World Wide Web for the expressed purpose of delivering marketing messages to attract customers by an ad server. 
Pop-up ads or pop-ups are often forms of online advertising on the World Wide Web intended to attract web traffic or capture email addresses.
A web banner or banner ad is a form of advertising on the World Wide Web.


Commercial advertising media can include, billboards,  printed flyers, radio, television adverts, web banners, mobile telephone screens,  skywriting, newspapers, bus stop benches, human billboards and forehead advertising, magazines, roof mounts

Television advertising / Music in advertising
The TV commercial during popular TV events is generally considered the most effective mass-market advertising format.

Infomercials
Infomercials describe, display, and often demonstrate products and their features, and commonly have testimonials from consumers and industry professionals.

New media
Technological development and economic globalization favors the emergence of new and new communication channels and new techniques of commercial messaging.

Other Ads
sides of buses, banners attached to or sides of airplanes ("logojets"),in-flight advertisements on seatback tray tables or overhead storage bins, taxicab doors, and passenger screens, musical stage shows, subway platforms and trains, stickers on apples in supermarkets, shopping cart handles (grabertising).
